Welcome to Dough Joy!

We're Seattle's specialty donut shop offering delicious and creative handmade donuts daily.

The Dough Joy story is a sweet one! From a food truck in May of 2021, to three shops in just three years, we're so proud of what we've built alongside our team and incredible customers.

While you wouldn't know it, all our donuts are egg free, dairy free, and nut free. We also offer gluten-free donuts as well! *not a gluten-free facility*

We are proudly an LGBTQ owned business and all are welcome.

Donuts for all.

As Seattle's specialty donut shop, we are proud to offer over 20 creative flavors daily, full coffee bar, and allergen friendly options for all.

At Dough Joy we aim to raise the bar with our friendly team, vibrant atmosphere, and delicious donuts.

We cut out all the animal products, use plant based ingredients, and make all our donuts in small batches daily to ensure an incredible donut experience.

We think we do donuts better, but you be the judge!
